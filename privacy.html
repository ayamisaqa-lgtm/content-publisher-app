Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the application "Content Publisher App".

The application "Content Publisher App" allows users to connect their TikTok account and upload videos using the TikTok API.

The application "Content Publisher App" does not store personal user data.

The application "Content Publisher App" only uses TikTok authentication and access tokens to perform actions requested by the user.

All actions in the "Content Publisher App" are initiated by the user.
