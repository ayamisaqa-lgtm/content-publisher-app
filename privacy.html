Privacy Policy

This application uses the TikTok API.

Information We Collect

We collect only the data necessary to publish content to a user's TikTok account, including access tokens provided via TikTok OAuth.

How We Use Information

Collected data is used solely to perform content publishing actions requested by the user.

Data Retention

We do not store personal data permanently. Tokens are used temporarily and discarded after the request is completed.
